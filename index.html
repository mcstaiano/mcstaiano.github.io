about

What you should know about me?

I am currently pursuing a PhD in Machine Translation and Language Technologies at the University of Macerata, Italy.

My doctoral research focuses on evaluating Machine Translation (MT) and Large Language Models (LLMs) within crisis communication and other high-stakes domains, such as public health.
